Services

We provide full-service wedding planning and day-of coordination services, and will work with you to figure out which package suits you best. We will determine your specific needs and make sure that all portions of your wedding, including the rehearsal, ceremony, and reception, go off without a hitch. We can provide the following services:

  • Day-of-coordination
  • Full service wedding ceremony & reception planning
  • Wedding events planning, including Rehearsal Dinner, Day After Brunch, and Honeymoon/Travel
  • Budget analysis
  • Event design
  • Guest list management

  • Vendor referrals

Pricing

Prices vary based on package and services needed. We are happy to adjust and work within your budget!

For day-of coordination, a basic package is $800 and would include:

  • Two 1hr consultations (in person or virtual, depending on location)
  • Unlimited email communication
  • Pre-wedding coordination with vendors (confirm timelines, answer questions, etc.)
  • Assistance with ceremony rehearsal and walk through of site week of wedding
  • On-site day-of coordination and point of contact for vendors, attendees, timeline, and all logistics (up to 6 hours - additional hours extra)

For full planning services, a basic package is $1500 and would include:

  • Everything outlined in the 'day-of' package (above) PLUS
  • Additional planning consultations to select vendors, design ceremony/reception flow, and help plan all of the little details with you!
  • Creation of timelines and to-do lists
  • Budget analysis
