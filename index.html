S&S Planning

San Francisco, CA · staceypalas@gmail.com

S&S Planning is a wedding planning service based in San Francisco, California that will coordinate all the details of your special day so that you don’t have a worry in the world! With attention to detail and clear communication, they will create a seamless wedding experience for any couple in the Bay Area.

Services

S&S Planning provides full-service wedding planning and day-of coordination, working with each couple to figure out which package suits them best. They will determine your specific needs and make sure that all portions of your wedding, including the rehearsal, ceremony, and reception, go off without a hitch. These are the services that they provide:

  • Budget analysis
  • Event design
  • Guest list management
  • Timelines
  • To-do lists
  • Vendor referrals
